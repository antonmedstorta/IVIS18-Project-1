Skill Vizualisation

Each bubble represents a different person. The size of the bubble represents their skill level in a certain area.
You're free to choose what skill you would like the size of the bubbles to represent.
The bigger the bubble, the more skillful the person claims to be in that area.

Hover over individual bubbles to see who that bubble represents.
Click individual bubbles to see that user's full set of skills.
